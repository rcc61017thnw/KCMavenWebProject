WHY RED CLAY?

WE UNDERSTAND UTILITIES

Red Clay focuses exclusively on the utility industry. Over the last 20 years, Red Clay has delivered more than 300 projects to utility companies of every size and type in the U.S. and across the globe. Experience with multi-service utilities in both regulated and competitive markets arms Red Clay with the insight to transform our clients’ technology and exceed their business needs.

WE KNOW ORACLE

Red Clay is a Gold-Level Oracle Partner focused on Oracle Utilities applications and technology platforms. Red Clay designs the best adoption strategies based on our clients’ unique needs. Our Oracle experience and relationship fuels these strategies.

WE ARE EXPERTS

Red Clay employs the largest and most-certified US-based Oracle Utilities implementation team. This Oracle technical knowledge combined with selective hiring, training, and development practices results in a deep understanding of meter-to-cash and customer experience best practices.

Red Clay team members receive extensive training in Red Clay’s TransformOne Methodology (T1). The T1 Methodology was built using Red Clay’s experience from 40+ full-scale implementations, hundreds of smaller engagements, and years working with Oracle Utilities products.
